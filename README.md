## 🎯 Project Overview

This is a **consulting-style business case study** on Zomato India — analyzing its financial performance, market position, unit economics, and strategic growth levers. It replicates the type of analysis done by McKinsey, BCG, Deloitte, and equity research teams.

**Business Problem:** Zomato went public in 2021 amid losses. How did it turn around to profitability? What are the key growth drivers, risks, and strategic opportunities for the next 3 years?

## 🔍 Analysis Framework

### 1. Financial Performance (FY22–FY24)
- Quarterly GMV, Revenue, and EBITDA trend analysis
- Identified the **profitability inflection point** (Q4 FY23)
- Revenue CAGR of ~72% over the analysis period

### 2. Market Position
- **55% market share** in India's food delivery market
- Duopoly with Swiggy — competitive moat analysis
- Blinkit acquisition as a strategic differentiator

### 3. Unit Economics Deep Dive
- Tracked per-order economics from FY22 to FY24
- Contribution per order: **-₹3 → +₹43** (₹46 improvement)
- Driven by take rate expansion + discount reduction

### 4. SWOT Analysis
| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|
| 55% market share | Path to profitability complex |
| Hyperpure B2B supply chain | High CAC in Tier-2 cities |
| Blinkit quick commerce moat | Top-8 city concentration |

| Opportunities | Threats |
|---|---|
| ₹1.4T food TAM by 2030 | ONDC government platform |
| Tier-2/3 city expansion | Swiggy price wars |
| Dining-out vertical | Restaurant bypass risk |

### 5. Growth Scenario Analysis (FY25–FY28)
| Scenario | FY28 GMV | Key Assumption |
|---|---|---|
| 🚀 Bull | ₹24,000 Cr | Tier-2 scale + new verticals |
| 📊 Base | ₹16,500 Cr | Steady market growth |
| ⚠️ Bear | ₹10,900 Cr | ONDC disruption + competition |

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

### Recommendation 1 — Tier-2 City Expansion 🏙️
Current top-8 metro concentration is a revenue risk. Tier-2 cities offer 2x TAM potential with lower real estate and delivery costs as brand awareness scales organically.

### Recommendation 2 — Take Rate Ceiling Watch ⚠️
Take rate has expanded from 20.8% → 27.8%. Restaurants may resist further hikes. Diversifying revenue via Zomato Gold memberships and ad revenue reduces this risk.

### Recommendation 3 — Double Down on Blinkit 🚀
Quick commerce (10-minute delivery) is growing faster than food delivery. Blinkit integration provides cross-sell opportunities and higher basket sizes.

### Recommendation 4 — ONDC Preparedness 🛡️
ONDC is a regulatory wildcard. Zomato should proactively build restaurant stickiness (Hyperpure, analytics tools) to reduce churn if ONDC gains traction.
